Help/Frequently Asked Questions

What is the burger?

The burger menu is accesed by tapping/clicking on the burger icon on the search and swipe pages. The burger menu is how you can apply filters such as cuisine, distance, and price.

What are "leftovers"?

Leftovers are the restaraunts that you swipe left on. this list can be modified or cleared in the settings tab, under "see leftovers."

What are "craves"?

craves are your liked restaraunts, and the restaraunts you like when you are swiping. these can be viewed on your profile.

how do I write a review?

click on the 3 dots in upper right corner of a craved restaraunt. select "write a review."

How do I change my username?

You can change your username in settings, under "account information." click on "edit", type in your new username, and click "save."

How to add things to your craves:

If you come across it while swiping, simply swipe right. from the search page, click the plus icon.

Where is the Logout button?

it's in the settings tab. big red letters that say "LOG OUT." Cant miss it.
